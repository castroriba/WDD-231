Membership Benefits Overview

NP Membership (Free)

For Non-Profit Organizations.

Bronze Membership

Entry-level access to events and discounts.

Silver Membership

Enhanced networking, training, and limited advertising.

Gold Membership

Premium visibility, guaranteed spotlight, and free advertising.

NP Membership Benefits

  • No annual membership fee.
  • Basic inclusion in the Chamber Directory.
  • Access to Non-Profit specific workshops.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Discounted pricing on all Chamber events and training.
  • Monthly business newsletter subscription.
  • Standard inclusion in the Chamber Directory.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits, plus:
  • Free attendance to two annual training seminars.
  • **Eligibility for Homepage Spotlights** (Rotation).
  • 50% discount on advertising space.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits, plus:
  • **Guaranteed** feature in the Homepage Spotlight (Rotation).
  • Free quarterly mentorship meetings with the Board.
  • 100% discount on all advertising space.
